About Lowara Safaris

Authentic Tanzania journeys designed with care, comfort, and unforgettable experiences.

Lowara Safaris is a Tanzania safari company based in Arusha, specializing in Serengeti safaris, Ngorongoro tours, Zanzibar holidays, and Kilimanjaro experiences. We create private and customized travel packages for couples, families, small groups, and travelers who want to experience Tanzania with confidence, clarity, and strong local support.

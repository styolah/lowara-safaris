Services

Full tourism support across Tanzania

Lowara Safaris is not just a safari listing page. We present ourselves as a complete tourism company able to support guests across many travel needs, with planning that feels organized and professional.

Wildlife Safaris: Carefully planned journeys to Serengeti, Ngorongoro, Tarangire, Lake Manyara, Arusha National Park, and other wildlife destinations for travelers who want classic Tanzania safari experiences.
Zanzibar Beach Holidays: Beach escapes designed for relaxation, romance, family leisure, and post-safari extensions with island stays, coastal experiences, and smooth transfers.
Kilimanjaro and Highland Trips: Scenic mountain views, day excursions, hiking add-ons, coffee tours, and northern Tanzania highland experiences for travelers seeking more than wildlife alone.
Airport Pickup and Transfers: Travel coordination from arrival to departure, including transport support, lodge transfer assistance, and organized movement between destinations.
Private, Group, Family, and Honeymoon Travel: Flexible travel styles that allow Lowara Safaris to serve different guest needs with care and confidence.
Custom Travel Design: Personalized itineraries shaped around dates, pace, destinations, comfort level, and special interests such as photography, romance, or cultural discovery.
